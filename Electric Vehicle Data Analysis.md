# Electric Vehicles Data Analysis Dashboard (Tableau)
Project Overview

The Electric Vehicles Data Analysis Dashboard is an interactive Tableau project designed to analyze Electric Vehicle (EV) adoption trends, vehicle distribution, manufacturer performance, and eligibility classifications across different states. This dashboard provides valuable insights into the growing EV market and helps stakeholders understand vehicle trends, market leaders, and geographical distribution.

Key Features
Total Electric Vehicle count and market overview.
Average Electric Range analysis.
State-wise EV distribution using an interactive map visualization.
CAFV (Clean Alternative Fuel Vehicle) Eligibility analysis.
Vehicle growth trend by model year.
Top vehicle manufacturers and market share analysis.
Detailed model-wise vehicle performance.
Dynamic filters for State, Make, Model, and CAFV Eligibility.
Dashboard Insights
Total Electric Vehicles Analyzed: 150,482
Average Electric Range: 67.88 Miles
Tesla dominates the EV market with more than 52% market share.
Battery Electric Vehicles (BEVs) represent the majority of registered EVs.
Significant growth in EV adoption is observed after 2020.
Model Y and Model 3 are the most popular electric vehicle models.
CAFV Eligible vehicles account for a large portion of total EV registrations.
